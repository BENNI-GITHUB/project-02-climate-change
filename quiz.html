About the Quiz!

What is the purpose of this quiz?

This quiz can be a fun and engaging way to educate people about the various aspects of climate change, including its causes, consequences, and potential solutions. They can help individuals become more informed and aware of the pressing issues related to climate change.

What are the rules of the quiz?

  • There are 10 questions with multiple choices in this quiz
  • You will have only 20 seconds per each question.
  • Once you selected your answer, you can't reselect.
  • You can't select any option once time goes off.
  • You can't exit from the Quiz while you're playing.
  • You'll get points on the basis of your correct answers.

What if some of your answers were wrong?

Dont Worry about that! We suggest you to read and research more about those issues.

Good luck!
